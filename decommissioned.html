PMI

PMI or also known as "PH7NTOM'S Modpack Installer" is a performance boosting modpack for Minecraft. It includes mods such as Lithium, Sodium, Iris Shaders, 3DSkinLayers, Simple-Discord-RPC and more. To view the full list of mods, download the credits.txt from the Github Repo!
